Overview

What Delegates Take Away

Model United Nations asks students to represent a position that isn't their own, and to argue it well. The skills built in committee outlast the conference.

01 — Diplomacy

Negotiation & Consensus

Delegates learn to build coalitions across disagreement, draft language that survives scrutiny, and find common ground under a ticking clock.

02 — Advocacy

Public Speaking

From formal speeches to unmoderated caucus, delegates learn to argue with structure, composure, and evidence in front of a critical room.

03 — Research

Policy & Analysis

Every position paper demands real research — into a country's history, alliances, and stated priorities on the issue at hand.

A Letter From the Secretaries-General

To Every Delegate Joining Us This October

It is our privilege to welcome you to Maria's Public School's Intra & Inter Model United Nations 2026. Over three days of committee session, negotiation, and resolution, we ask you to set aside easy certainty and sit with the difficulty of representing a position that may not be your own.

MUN rewards preparation, but it is decided in the room — in the amendment proposed at the right moment, in the bloc that holds together under pressure, in the delegate who listens as carefully as she speaks. We have built this conference to test exactly that.

To our first-time delegates: ask questions, make mistakes early, and trust the process. To our returning delegates: raise the bar for the room. To everyone — we are glad you are here.

With anticipation,
Ayushmita Das Choudhury

Formats

Intra & Inter MUN

Two formats, one standard of debate.

FORMAT 01

Intra MUN

Open to every delegate at Maria's Public School. A foundational committee experience — ideal for first-time delegates, and a proving ground for those returning to sharpen their procedure and push for a stronger position.

FORMAT 02

Inter MUN

Delegates from schools across the region convene under a single roof. Higher stakes, sharper competition, and committees judged against a wider field.

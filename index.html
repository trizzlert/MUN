Overview

What Delegates Take Away

Model United Nations asks students to represent a position that isn't their own, and to argue it well. The skills built in committee outlast the conference.

01 — Diplomacy

Negotiation & Consensus

Delegates learn to build coalitions across disagreement, draft language that survives scrutiny, and find common ground under a ticking clock.

02 — Advocacy

Public Speaking

From formal speeches to unmoderated caucus, delegates learn to argue with structure, composure, and evidence in front of a critical room.

03 — Research

Policy & Analysis

Every position paper demands real research — into a country's history, alliances, and stated priorities on the issue at hand.

A Letter From the Secretaries-General

To Every Delegate Joining Us This October

It is my profound privilege to welcome you to the tenth edition of the Maria's Model United Nations. & Being one of the first institutions to embark on the beginning of Model United Nations in the North East region, our school has never failed to continue the legacy of excellence since 2016.

Like our prior conferences, the tenth edition is set to be equally significant and exceptional. This conference will not only acknowledge your achievements but also serve as a platform where your ideas, perspectives, dialogues, and solutions are recognized and appreciated.

I encourage every participant to engage wholeheartedly, debate respectfully, and approach every discussion with an open mind. May Maria’s Model United Nations 2026 serve as a space where ideas are exchanged, voices are heard, and diplomacy inspires action..

I wish all delegates the very best and look forward to witnessing an enriching and memorable conference.
